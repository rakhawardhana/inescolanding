Shallots are closely related to onions, garlic, and chives. Whether diced, minced, or sliced, shallots are used for seasoning dishes, either with a soft onion undercurrent or a pop of sharp acidity similar to a hint of garlic.

  • Colour: Rose Red
    • Weight: 3 - 5 gram (Depends on Size)
    • Size: 3 - 5 cm
    • Origin: Indonesia - Brebes (Grade B)
